RYAN KEMERER

Videographer / Photographer / Drone Operator / Editor

To make a life out of the extreme, capture the world from above, and that which is dangerous to come to, that which we find to capture in our eyes. Snowboarding & content creation were my artistic outlet, and when I was 16, I started snowboard instructing on the weekends to get a free season pass. This, along with getting into the Chambersburg Area Career Magnet School, gave me a platform to learn about content creation and spend hours in the media center learning that craft.

Now, 10 years later, I moved to Colorado and started KemererMedia.com with a simple vision: to capture the world from above. As a certified FAA Part 107 drone pilot, I deliver precise, creative, and professional aerial content for businesses and individuals alike. From real estate and construction to storytelling projects, my mission is to give your work the elevated perspective it deserves.
